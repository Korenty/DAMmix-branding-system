Logo Branding Stress Tests

DAMmix logo is tested under multiple professional branding stress scenarios to ensure visibility, clarity, and consistent identity across all applications.

Visibility Test

Evaluates logo clarity on small-scale print, merchandise, and signage.

  • Small print: 95%
  • Merchandise: 90%
  • Large signage: 98%

Color Contrast Test

Tests readability against multiple background colors and textures.

  • Deep Navy background: 100%
  • White background: 100%

Scalability Test

Checks logo integrity from tiny icons to huge banners.

  • Icon-size: 95%

Print & Digital Reproduction Test

Assesses logo performance in CMYK, RGB, and online platforms.

  • CMYK print: 97%

Environmental Durability Test

Ensures logo visibility and integrity in real-world construction environments.

  • Outdoor signage: 95%
  • Weathered surfaces: 93%
  • High sunlight exposure: 94%
