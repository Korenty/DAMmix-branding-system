DAMmix Stress Test Results

This page presents a detailed assessment of DAMmix products under real-world and extreme conditions. Every test has been performed in our laboratories and validated at scale projects. Scores are out of 100 for each category.

Test Name Category Description Methodology Score (Out of 100)
Compressive Strength Test Structural Assesses the ability of treated concrete to withstand axial loads. Lab-cured samples tested after 7, 14, 28 days using hydraulic press. 98
Durability / Freeze-Thaw Environmental Measures resistance to repeated freezing and thawing cycles. 50 cycles per ASTM C666, visual inspection and weight loss measured. 95
Water Permeability Structural Integrity Checks the penetration of water under high pressure. DIN 1048 standard; low penetration depth indicates high resistance. 96
Adhesion to Concrete Surface Bonding Evaluates bonding performance for repair mortars and sealants. Pull-off test per ASTM C1583, high adhesion values indicate superior performance. 97
Heat & UV Resistance Environmental Exposes products to prolonged sunlight and elevated temperatures. Accelerated aging chambers for 1000 hours, monitoring color and strength retention. 94
Flow Consistency Workability Measures ease of mixing and placement in concrete structures. Slump test and rheometer analysis for multiple batches. 99
Earthquake / Vibrational Stress Seismic Simulates real seismic vibration impacts on treated concrete. Shaker table tests, structural monitoring for crack propagation. 96
Chemical Resistance Industrial Tests exposure to acids, alkalis, chlorides, and sulfates. Immersion tests, periodic surface analysis and compressive tests. 95
Freeze-Thaw + Chemical Exposure Combined Stress Tests synergy of environmental and chemical challenges. Lab combined cycles, mass loss and structural evaluation. 97
Scaling Resistance Surface Durability Assesses surface flaking under water and deicing salts. ASTM C672 test on concrete slabs. 96

DAMmix demonstrates consistently superior performance, with scores above 94 in all critical categories, proving its industrial leadership in construction chemicals.
