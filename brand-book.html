DAMmix Brand Book

Core Identity

The DAMmix brand is defined by precision, reliability, and industrial strength. Our identity includes the primary logotype, geometric icon, and grid system, adaptable to all digital and print formats.

Brand Colors

Our primary colors reinforce professionalism and industrial reliability:

  • Deep Navy: #0b1f3f – Authority, trust, technical mastery.
  • Industrial Red: #c1121f – Precision, energy, safety, innovation.
  • White / Gray: Support colors for clarity, contrast, and hierarchy.

Typography

We use modern, legible sans-serif fonts suitable for technical and corporate contexts. Font weights are leveraged to create hierarchy and readability across all applications.

Applications & Mockups

Logo Usage Guidelines

  • Maintain clear space equal to 1x logo height around all logo applications.
  • Do not distort or recolor the logo outside the approved color palette.
  • Use the geometric icon for pattern and watermark applications for brand recognition.

Print & Digital Guidelines

  • Maintain minimum DPI of 300 for all print materials.
  • Digital applications: RGB color space, optimized for web visibility and accessibility.
  • Ensure proper contrast for accessibility standards.

Packaging & Branding

All chemical drums, jerrycans, and mixers should display the primary logo prominently, with the icon patterns subtly applied as brand reinforcement.
