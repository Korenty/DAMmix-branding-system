DAMmix Brand Book

The comprehensive guide to DAMmix visual identity, application, and brand philosophy for consistent global representation.

Logo Usage

Primary logo, monochrome, negative, and icon versions are available. Always maintain clear space around the logo, never distort or recolor outside brand palette.

Color Palette

Main colors: Deep Navy Blue #0b1f3f, Industrial Red #c1121f, with neutrals Gray and White for balance. These colors are used for logos, typography, backgrounds, and interface elements.

Typography

Primary typeface: Inter / Helvetica Neue / Arial. Use bold for headings, regular for body copy. Maintain spacing consistency for readability across all materials.

Applications & Mockups

Consistent logo placement, typography, and color usage ensures the DAMmix identity remains professional, credible, and instantly recognizable across all touchpoints.

Applications Guidelines

  • Always maintain minimum clear space around the logo.
  • Use brand colors consistently. Never modify the logo color outside defined palette.
  • Use the geometric icon subtly as pattern or watermark on packaging and documents.
  • Product labeling, packaging, and machinery branding must always follow layout proportions defined in the brand grid.
  • All images must be high-resolution and consistent with brand style.

Brand Philosophy

DAMmix is a laboratory-driven brand built on innovation, technical mastery, and reliability. The "Build Without Limits" ethos informs design, R&D, and product application, ensuring the brand is synonymous with excellence, precision, and trust in the construction chemicals industry.

Digital & Print Applications

The DAMmix brand can be applied to websites, documents, marketing collateral, vehicle wraps, machinery, safety gear, and product packaging. Every application should reflect professionalism, authority, and technical excellence.
