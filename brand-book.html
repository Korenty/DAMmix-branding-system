Usage Guidelines

  • Always maintain clear space around the logo equal to the height of the geometric diamond icon.
  • Do not distort, rotate, or recolor the logo outside of official palettes.
  • Use high-resolution versions for print; digital versions must be optimized but retain clarity.
  • Patterns should be low opacity and never overpower the primary logo.
  • Ensure visibility on all backgrounds; use negative or monochrome versions as required.
