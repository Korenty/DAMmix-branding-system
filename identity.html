DAMmix Corporate Identity

DAMmix's corporate identity is crafted to convey scientific precision, industrial reliability, and modern construction excellence. Our logo, color palette, typography, and applications create a cohesive visual language across all touchpoints.

Logo Anatomy

The DAMmix logo is a **combined abstract icon and wordmark**. The icon features a **checkmark flowing through a red line**, representing construction limits or unforeseen constraints that are overcome without disruption, emphasizing our lab-tested scientific approach.
